Awareness Hub
💡 Know Your Threats
Understanding how phishing works is your best defense. Explore the tactics attackers use.
Most Common
📧
Email Phishing
Fraudulent emails impersonate banks, tech companies, or colleagues to steal credentials. Always verify the full sender domain before clicking anything.
High Risk
🔗
Malicious Links
Typosquatted URLs (paypa1.com) hide dangerous destinations. Hover every link before clicking and scan suspicious URLs at VirusTotal.com.
Rising Threat
📱
Smishing (SMS)
Fake texts from delivery services or banks. Never click unsolicited SMS links — go directly to official websites by typing the address yourself.
Social Attack
🎭
Social Engineering
Psychological manipulation exploiting urgency, authority, and fear. These attacks bypass technology by targeting human psychology.
Targeted
🎯
Spear Phishing
Personalized attacks using your name and info from social media. Feels legitimate because it's designed specifically for you.
Corporate
👔
BEC / CEO Fraud
Email impersonates your CEO to authorize wire transfers. Always verbally verify unusual financial requests — email alone is never enough.
